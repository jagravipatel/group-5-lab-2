Chapter 3: Modular origami crafts work

Modular origami or unit origami is a paperfolding technique which uses two or more sheets of paper to create a larger and more complex structure than would be possible using single-piece origami techniques.

  1. Fish

    2. Origami is an art of folding paper sheets and for making fish various colors are used and are folded into three-dimension.

Each individual sheet of paper is folded into a module, or unit, and then modules are assembled into an integrated flat shape or three-dimensional structure by inserting flaps into pockets created by the folding process.
